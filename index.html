How It Works

You have a business idea you want to test out. We will build an minimal viable product (MVP) in as little as two weeks for the price of $5000.

Our process involves:

  1. An initial meeting where we understand the business concept and the scope of the MVP.
  2. A second meeting there we will mockup the screens that need to be built together, to ensure that we are on the same page, and ambiguities we come across will be resolved early.
  3. We start building, giving you a deployed and demoable app at the end of each day of work.
  4. At the end of the 2-week build process, we are done, and you own the product and code we produced.
